Triggers & Drips
Send automatically when a contact enters a Status, Stage, Touch step, or Segment — or when a CRM event fires (form submitted, stage action logged, tag applied, or deliverability event). Each trigger is a sequence of steps (template + delay) — a drip. For Status/Stage/Segment triggers, Activate enrolls every currently-matching contact now. For event triggers, enrollment happens automatically in production when the CRM event fires; suppression + freq cap are always honored at send time.
Deliverability & Testing
Pre-flight an email before it sends: a content spam-score, an SPF/DKIM/DMARC readiness check, and a subject-line A/B open-rate test. Scoring runs entirely here; live seed-inbox placement and DNS/blocklist lookups route to the backend via the API.

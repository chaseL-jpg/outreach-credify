Everything your front office runs on

One toolkit for the operational side of a behavioral health agency — from the first referral to the paid claim.

Cluster Email outreach

Send templated, tracked email to whole segments of referral partners and community contacts — with open rates, best-send-hour insights, and per-recipient delivery status.

Referral CRM

Track intakes, referral sources, and care-coordination contacts by type and status — with assigned reps, tasks, and follow-up cadences that keep nothing slipping.

Provider credentialing

Keep every clinician's payer enrollments, licenses, and renewal dates in one tracked pipeline — no more spreadsheet archaeology when a payer asks for proof.

Billing & RCM

Clean claims out the door and denials worked fast. Authorization tracking and clear invoicing keep revenue moving without adding headcount.

Clinical form builder

Standard screeners — PHQ-9, GAD-7, PCL-5, AUDIT and more — as clean digital forms your intake team can send, score, and file in minutes.

Worklists & scheduling

Daily task worklists for front-office staff, tied to contacts and deadlines — so authorizations, renewals, and follow-ups happen on time, every time.
